About CleanITR

Making tax simple, accessible & useful.

A practical, easy-to-use tax platform built to simplify income tax for real-life situations — not just theory.

CleanITR is backed by a team of qualified Chartered Accountants with hands-on experience in taxation, compliance and financial reporting. Instead of operating like a traditional CA firm, CleanITR focuses on making tax knowledge more accessible, understandable, and useful for everyday financial decisions.

We understand that tax rules can often feel confusing, technical, and overwhelming — especially for individuals managing their own finances. That's why everything on CleanITR is designed with simplicity and clarity in mind. Here's what we focus on:

Clear explanations

Clear, no-jargon explanations that are easy to understand — no assumed knowledge needed.

Practical tools

Free calculators and utilities for everyday tax calculations — built to be used, not just browsed.

Real-world insights

Guidance shaped by professional experience — what actually matters in practice, not just theory.

All content and tools available on this platform are carefully reviewed from a professional perspective to ensure accuracy, relevance, and reliability.
