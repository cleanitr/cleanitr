Good to know

Questions people ask us most.

What documents do I need to file my ITR?
For most salaried individuals: Form 16, bank statements, details of any other income, and proofs of tax-saving investments (like 80C and 80D). Not sure what applies to you? Just email what you have and we'll guide you on the rest.
How long does the whole process take?
Once we have your documents, a straightforward return is usually reviewed and ready for your approval within a couple of working days. Complex cases with capital gains or business income may take a little longer.
Do I have to visit your office?
Not at all. Everything can be done online over email. But if you prefer meeting in person, you're welcome at any of our three offices in Delhi, Saharanpur or Yamunanagar.
Is my financial data safe with you?
Yes. Your documents are handled confidentially by our team and are never shared with anyone outside the firm. We treat your data with the same care we'd expect for our own.
What if I get a tax notice later?
We've got you covered. Forward the notice to us and a CA will review it and draft the appropriate response. Handling notices and replies is one of our core services.
How much do you charge?
Fees depend on the complexity of your return and the services you need. Share your requirement with us and we'll give you a clear, upfront quote before any work begins — no surprises.
