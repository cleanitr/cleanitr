GST Calculator

Add GST to a base amount (exclusive) or extract GST from a total (inclusive) — with an automatic CGST + SGST split at every slab rate.

Who should use this

Shopkeepers, freelancers and businesses preparing invoices or checking whether a quoted price is GST-inclusive.

Example

₹10,000 service @ 18% → GST ₹1,800 (CGST ₹900 + SGST ₹900) → invoice total ₹11,800.
