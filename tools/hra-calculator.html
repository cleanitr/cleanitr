HRA Exemption Calculator

Under Section 10(13A), the exempt HRA is the least of three amounts. Enter your annual figures and we'll show the full working.

Who should use this

Salaried employees who receive HRA and pay rent — and want to claim the correct exemption under the old regime.

Example

Basic ₹6L, HRA received ₹2.4L, rent ₹3L in Delhi → exemption is the least of the three amounts shown in the working above.
