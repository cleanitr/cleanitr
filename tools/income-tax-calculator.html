Income Tax Calculator

FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27). Enter your income once — we compute both regimes, apply standard deduction, rebate and cess, and tell you which regime saves more.

Who should use this

Salaried individuals, pensioners and freelancers deciding between the old and new regime — especially anyone unsure whether their 80C/80D investments still make the old regime worth it.

Example

₹12,75,000 salary → New regime tax: ₹0 (Section 87A rebate applies). Old regime: depends on your deductions — enter them above and compare.

Calculations use FY 2025-26 slabs: new regime — nil up to ₹4L, 5% (4–8L), 10% (8–12L), 15% (12–16L), 20% (16–20L), 25% (20–24L), 30% above ₹24L, standard deduction ₹75,000 and Section 87A rebate for taxable income up to ₹12L. Old regime — standard deduction ₹50,000, rebate up to ₹5L taxable. Surcharge and marginal relief not included. For exact computation, consult our CAs.
