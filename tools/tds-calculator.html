TDS Calculator

Pick the payment type, enter the amount, and get the TDS to deduct along with the applicable section, rate and annual threshold.

Who should use this

Businesses and individuals paying rent, contractor bills, professional fees or commission who must deduct TDS before payment.

Example

₹60,000/month office rent → Section 194I @ 10% → deduct ₹6,000, pay ₹54,000 to the landlord.

Rates shown assume the payee has furnished PAN (without PAN, TDS applies at 20% under Section 206AA). Thresholds are per FY 2025-26 and apply annually unless stated. This is an estimate — actual liability may vary; confirm with our CAs before deducting.
